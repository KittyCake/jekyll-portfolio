About

Hi, I am Chen Sz Ting, a Full-stack developer skilled in Ruby on rails, Front end development and UI design. I graduated 2 years ago from National Central University in Management Information system department. After graduation, I worked as a product assistant in an investment company for 2 years. I have learned how to finish the work more efficiently and the way to collaborate with my partner.

In September 2018, I joined Le Wagon intensive full stack bootcamp and finished the course in December 2018. I am a native Chinese Mandarine speaker and fluent in speaking/writing English. Le Wagon course is fully based in English with international classmates. During the course, I learned either language and teamwork. I worked very hard day and night, and spent most of my time doing the project and self-learning. The most precious ability I got from the class is how to search for the answer and solve the problems.

With passion and responsibility, I take every project seriously and put all my heart into it. I can build responsive website, do back end development, and create WeChat mini program. Please check my CV as the attachment, my Github link (https://github.com/KittyCake) and my portfolio website (https://chenszting.com/) for further information. Thank you! I look forward to you response.
